Jessica is a great language teacher and Chinese is a fun language to learn. I have only spent about ~40 hours over the last 8 months studying Chinese but in that time I have become very comfortable reading and writing Chinese. A few things that I like about her lessons compared to other language classes I have been in: -I have spoken in person to dozens of Chinese people and Jessica is by far the best at understanding my atrocious foreigner accent. You'd be surprised how many people either don't understand or pretend not to understand if you don't get the tone exactly right. -She is very flexible with scheduling/communication. Since I am busy with work and travel this is a huge plus. -She will teach you whatever you want. Slang? Travel? Food? No problem. But if you have nothing in mind, she can draw up a suitable lesson plan. -I am pretty shy/introverted so I appreciate the relaxed environment and friendly conversation, as opposed to being in a large class.

David S.

Before I started taking classes at Bay Area Chinese Language School, I tried to learn Chinese by myself for about one year. I tried textbooks, YouTube and audiobooks. None worked well for me so I started taking Jessica's lessons. It has been about six months and I made far more progress than the previous year. For me, the most difficult thing about learning Chinese is the pronunciations. And that is where Jessica is really good at teaching. Her standard is very high and she doesn't easily say OK if it is not perfect. I think that is exactly what you need when you want to be able to speak other languages. The class is fun and she can answer any questions not only the language but other things such as Chinese culture and lifestyle. I highly recommend her to anyone who is serious about learning the Chinese language.
