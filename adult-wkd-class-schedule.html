Mandarin Level 1

Mandarin Level 1 is a beginner comprehensive course, designed for students who have very little to no experience with the Mandarin language. No knowledge of Chinese characters is required to take this level.

In this level you will learn:
  • Pinyin: Consonants, Vowels and Tones
  • Topics: Greeting, Self Introduction, Family, Numbers

Students will be able to have a basic introduction of themselves and other people as well as ask basic questions with simple daily topics.
