  • Registeration

  • How do I register for a class?

    If you are interested in registering a private class, please click Register or use our contact form. Usually within 24-48 hours, you will receive a reply to confirm your lesson.

  • Do you offer a 30 mins trial lesson?

    We do not offer trial lessons at this time. We offer a 2 1-hour Welcome Package at a very affordable price to let students try out our lessons.

  • Do you offer Semi-Private lessons? (for example, siblings, friends, work groups learning in small groups)

    We offer Semi-Private lessons. Please click Register to book your lesson.

  • Can I reserve a seat for a group class?

    The seat is reserved upon the payment.

  • Payments

  • How much are the classes?

    The class price depends on the class type, we have Private, Semi-Private and Small Group classes available starting at $22/hr.

  • What type of payments do you accept?

    We currently accept the following payment methods:

    • Cash
    • Check
    • PayPal
    • Venmo
    • Zelle

  • When are payments due?

    The tuition for a single Private lesson is expected after your class.

    We recommend students taking package lessons. This way, your prefered time slot is guaranteed, and it will be more cost-effective (see next question)

  • Do you offer discounts for package lessons?

    We do offer discounts for package lessons. Please see Pricing.

  • Cancellation

  • Group Class Cancellations

    If a class is cancelled by BACLS for any reason, students may switch to another class offered or be refunded in full.

  • What happens if I miss a class or session?

    If you miss a group class, you will not receive a free makeup class. We offer a 30 minute makeup session (a review of material covered in missed class) for $15. This makeup session is completely optional.

  • What happens if I cancel my Private lesson?

    You may reschedule your private lesson at no charge with a minimum of 24 hour notice. In the case of a no-show lesson, the lesson will be considered taken and you will be charged the full cost of the lesson.

  • What if the Private lesson is canceled within 24 hours?

    If the lesson is canceled within 24 hours, a $15 late cancellation fee will be applied.

  • Refunds & Postponements

  • What happens if I withdraw from a program?
    • The group class will only be refunded in full if the course withdrawal is prior to the first day of class.
    • A $50 fee applies to cancellation or postponements during the first week of the first day of the class.
    • No refunds, postponements, prorating or credits are allowed once classes begin, regardless of any reason.
