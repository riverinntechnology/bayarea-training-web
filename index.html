Lesson for Adult

Private 1-on-1 lessons are the quickest and most cost-effective way to improve your Chinese skills. We personalize your lessons according to your needs and level.

We teach every level, from beginner through advanced. Whether you would learn immersively or only learn how to speak without learning Chinese characters, we will tailor the content for you in order to help you reach your goals faster.

    Our private lessons are designed for people who

  • Travel to China frequently on business

  • Have Chinese colleagues at work

  • Choose Chinese as a Second Language at school
