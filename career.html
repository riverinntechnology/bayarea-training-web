Afterschool Mandarin & Math Instructor

We are looking for a passionate individual who wants to help spread Chinese language and culture.

Qualifications:
  • Native Mandarin speaker without any accent
  • Communicate very well in English
  • Previous Experience in other after-schools
  • Mandarin teaching experience is a must
  • Good people skills
  • Willing to learn and to-do attitude
  • Need to be very punctual and enthusiastic
  • Must be legal to work
  • Degree in Chinese language preferred
  • Be able to coordinate fun and cultural activities
Duties may include but are not limited to:
  • Teach a group of students in a classroom environment with pre-defined curriculum
  • Teach one or more students in a private or semi-private environment
  • Create lesson plans and materials
  • Create fun and engaging curriculum
  • Maintain a safe, healthy and happy learning environment
  • Other duties as assigned
Compensation:
  • $18-22 per hour DOE
  • Working hours: Monday to Friday: 3pm - 6:30pm
